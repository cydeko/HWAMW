# Flavor Text — Path Choices

Small narrative forks between data-gathering sections. Purely cosmetic — all choices lead to the same next step. Log responses for qualitative texture but do not use to gate or branch the experience.

---

## Fork 1 — The River vs. The Trees
*Placement: between Hook and List of Troubles*

> The path divides. To the left, you can just make out the glint of water through the reeds — a river, or something like one. To the right, the trees close in, and the ground looks soft enough to swallow a boot.

- [ Follow the water ]
- [ Take your chances with the trees ]

---

## Fork 2 — The Puddle
*Placement: between List of Troubles and List of Qualities*

> There's a puddle across the path. Not a small one. You pause, calculating. There's a dry route around it, along a narrow strip of grass, or there's just… going through. You're already up late and trespassing, probably. A wet boot seems beside the point.

- [ Go around ]
- [ Go through ]

---

## Fork 3 — The Bench
*Placement: between List of Qualities and First Look*

> Someone has left a bench here, at the side of the path. Old wood, a little warped. It has the look of a thing that is mostly sat on by people who need a moment to talk themselves into continuing.

- [ Sit for a moment ]
- [ Keep moving ]

---

## Fork 4 — The Outbuilding
*Placement: between First Look and the Gatekeeper*

> Just before the gate, there's a small outbuilding — stone, older than it looks. The door is open. A light inside, warm and still. You weren't going to stop, and then you were.

- [ Look inside ]
- [ Walk past ]
